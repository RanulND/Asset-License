Medical Exams


Please bring the following documents before coming for the checkup.


National Identity Card.
Glasses and prescription, If applicable.
Medical information regarding any illness you may have had.
A list of all medications you are currently taking.
Police and court documents regarding any criminal history.


Written Exams


Please bring the following documents before coming for the written exam.



National Identity Card.
The original and a photocopy of the Birth Certificate.
Vaccination card or Medical certificate.
Three copies of black and white photos of size: 2*2 ”.
A Certification of Competency issued by a grade “A” registered driving school.
