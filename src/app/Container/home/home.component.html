About Us

Asset License is a website designed for your convenience to alleviate the daily congestion and busyness by digitizing the majority of the licensing process with DMT. Check your license history and find the status of your license through us. Join us in the process of getting your license in your hand anytime, anywhere. At its most basic, An Asset License online booking system that allows you to book and check for an activity or service directly through a website.
